About The WCIF Database

This Database is a collection of the CIF genes found in Wolbachia and provides information in regards to their collection.

The data is searchable by geneID, strain, and city of collection.

Founder & Contributors

This Database was founded by Jesse Valliere in collaboration with Sarah Bordenstein, M.S., and the Bordenstein Lab at Vanderbilt University, under the supervision of Dr. Ash Stuart at Ramapo College of New Jersey.
